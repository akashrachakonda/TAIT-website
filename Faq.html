FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

General questions

What is TAIT?

TAIT is a platform which is provided by department of IT to showcase the skills of students.

Who are eligible?

Students who belong to IT department are eligible for all the events.

How to participate?

You can participate in the events by registeration through online.

Event Results Declaration?

Event results will be displayed within 2 days after conducting the event.

Is participation in TAIT events mandatory?

Its quite simple. If you want to be in the race then participate in all the events. You got a chance then grab it!

What are the benefits of TAIT?

The following benefits are there to the students:
  • Successful students get certificates.
  • Top students details list will be displayed in the TAIT website.
  • Awards and rewards to top students at the end of each semester.
  • Provides platform to test student knowledge in various events at department level.
  • Provides interaction with faculty and other students and more...
